Vegan Fast Feed is the app helping you to make the newest recipes from your favorite vegan RSS feeds. You'll find it easy as vegan pie to keep track of the ingredients you have at home in order to call up Vegan Fast Feed when you need this info the most: at your local farmer's market or the grocery store!

Vegan Fast Feed compares what you've got in your stash to the newest vegan RSS feed recipes and then sends you the 3 best matches with a list of the ingredients you still need to buy. The recipes with cooking instructions and photos are, of course, saved automatically to your account!

Vegan Fast Feed makes your life easier (and more delicous!), facilitates an animal-friendly, eco-friendly lifestyle, and will probably end up saving the planet.

FAQ

Can I use Vegan Fast Feed even if I'm not a vegan?
Yes, you can, but only if you love delicious food, cooking-convenience, and saving the planet! Whether you're a hardcore-vegan, a sometimes-vegan, a vegetarian, or just want to try your hand at eco-friendly, mouth-watering cuisine, Vegan Fast Feed is for you.
Which blogs and RSS feeds are featured by Vegan Fast Feed?
That's where you come in: vote in our poll for the vegan feeds you've just got to have, and see (d)e-mocracy in progress! There's a direct link to each blog on the polling site, so that you can browse pages of scrumptious recipes, get to know some of the most awesome vegan cooks online, and start getting hungry right now!
Do you plan on someday expanding Vegan Fast Feed in order to include non-vegan recipes?
No. There are already numerous recipe-apps out there that provide this feature. Vegan Fast Feed, however, is specialised for the hottest *vegan* recipes online now, and commited to an animal-friendly lifestyle.
How much does Vegan Fast Feed cost?
Vegan Fast Feed costs 10€ for a one-year subscription. Profits will be donated to the animal shelter in Berlin, Germany. The Vegan Fast Feed philosphy aims at increasing awareness of how delicious and simple vegan food can be, while shining a light on how awesome our favorite vegan bloggers are! In the process, money gets raised that will directly help out abandoned animals in need. With Vegan Fast Feed, everybody wins.
