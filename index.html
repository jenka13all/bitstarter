Vegan Fast Feed is an app that helps you grocery-shop for the newest recipes from the hottest vegan RSS feeds: Post Punk Kitchen, Oh She Glows, and more. Vegan Fast Feed makes your life easier (and more delicous!), facilitates an eco-friendly lifestyle, and will probably end up saving the planet.

Vegan Fast Feed uses Evernote as the place where you keep track of what you've currently got in your kitchen, so when you're at the store getting groceries, all you have to do is call it up. Vegan Fast Feed will compare what you've got in your stash with the ingredients of the newest recipes from your favorite vegan blog RSS feeds and then send you the 3 best matches, along with a list of the ingredients you still need to buy. The recipes with cooking instructions and photos are, of course, saved automatically to your Evernote account!

FAQ

Can I use Vegan Fast Feed even if I'm not a vegan?
Yes, you can, but only if you love delicious food and cooking-convenience! Whether you're a hardcore-vegan, a sometimes-vegan, a vegetarian, or just want to try your hand at novel, mouth-watering healthy cuisine, Vegan Fast Feed is for you.
What if I don't like a particular vegan RSS feed offered by Vegan Fast Feed?
Vegan Fast Feed allows you to remove RSS Feeds from the offered selection.
Do you plan on someday expanding Vegan Fast Feed in order to include non-vegan recipes?
No. There are already numerous recipe-apps out there that provide this feature. Vegan Fast Feed, however, is specialised for the hottest new vegan recipes available online.
